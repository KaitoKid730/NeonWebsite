Our Neon Story

For over 15 years, NeonGlow Studios has been transforming visions into vibrant neon realities. Our journey began with a passion for light and has evolved into creating iconic installations worldwide.

Our Mission

At NeonGlow Studios, we believe in the transformative power of light. Our mission is to create custom neon experiences that captivate, inspire, and illuminate spaces with artistic brilliance.

Founded in 2008 by master neon artisan James Carter, we've grown from a small workshop to an internationally recognized studio. Our team of 25 artists, designers, and technicians bring diverse skills to every project.

We blend traditional glass bending techniques with cutting-edge LED technology to create sustainable, vibrant installations that stand the test of time.
